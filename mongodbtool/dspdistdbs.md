dspdistdbs
==========
distdb内のテーブル一覧を表示

## 使い方

```ruby
mongo = Num4DistDBLIB::Num4DistDBInfoLib.new
ret = mongo.dspdistdbs
ret.each do |name|
    puts name
end
```
